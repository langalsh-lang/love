✨ Happy Anniversary ✨

It all started on January 20, 2020… A normal day, a normal biochemistry class… Nothing felt special at that moment. But somehow… that was the first time I saw you. And without even realizing… something inside me quietly changed.
We started talking slowly… just random conversations at first. But little by little… those small talks became my favorite part of the day. And before I knew it… you became a part of my everyday life.
Then came March 31… A night that felt like everything was falling apart. We fought like we were losing each other forever. It hurt… more than anything.
But the very next day… April 1… We chose each other again. Not out of habit… but because we truly wanted to stay. And that meant everything.
And today… after all these years… After every fight, every storm… we are still here. Still choosing each other… again and again.
You showed up for me when it mattered the most… Hospital visits… silent support… You didn’t need to say anything. Your presence alone meant everything to me.
When I was flying to India… You followed my whole journey… every update, every moment… As if your heart was travelling with me.
You travelled 24 hours… just to spend 6 hours with me. And somehow… that says everything about us.
That moment… when I surprised you at the door… Your reaction… it was priceless. I wish I could pause that moment and live in it a little longer.
Last year… our anniversary wasn’t easy. You had duty from 7 am to 7 pm, and mine was 7 pm to 7 am… We didn’t even get to see each other. But before you came home… I made sure to leave a little surprise for you — An Apple Pencil… and a small decoration filled with love. Every bit of that effort… was just for you.
And your birthday… That resort surprise… was the best thing I could think of for you. We laughed, we celebrated… surrounded by the best people. That day… felt perfect.
Somehow… I always manage to surprise you 😄 Maybe that’s just my way of loving you.
And you… You never needed big surprises. Because you always knew what I needed… And gave me everything — before I even asked. That’s the kind of love I fell for.
But that day… April 1, 2024… You completely surprised me. The decoration… the iPad… But more than that — the thought behind it. That you understood me so deeply… I fell for you all over again.
Before going further… Just pause for a moment. Think about us… every memory… every feeling… Everything we’ve been through.
There are so many moments… big and small… But every single one of them… is a reason I stayed. I never stopped choosing you… not even once.

Will you stay forever? 💍
